Scenarios

Karen - "How Can I Find Local Sales?"

Karen navigates to the "Promotions" page of the site and peruses her current options for what's been posted. She can click on each business listed to find their telephone numbers to call them herself for more information.

Albert - "How Do I Get New Clientele?"

Albert navigates to the "Contact Us" page to correspond with the webmaster to add a new entry to the "Promotions" page of the site. Don't worry, Albert. Karen will be there, if no one else.

Karen - "What Attractions Could I Visit With My Grandchildren?"

Karen navigates to the "Find a Business" search bar and proceeds to type in "children", "fun", or "family" and gets a comprehensive list of options, including participating bowling alleys, ice-cream shops, and movie theaters.
